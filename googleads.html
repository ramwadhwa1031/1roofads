What are Google Ads?

Google Ads, formerly known as Google AdWords, is an advertising platform provided by Google that allows businesses to display ads on Google's search engine results pages (SERPs) and across its advertising network. It operates on a pay-per-click (PPC) model, where advertisers bid on keywords and pay for clicks on their ads.

How do Google Ads work?

Google Ads work by allowing advertisers to create text, display, and video ads that appear on Google's search results and partner websites. Advertisers select keywords relevant to their products or services, set a budget, and bid on those keywords. When users search for those keywords, Google displays relevant ads, and advertisers pay when users click on their ads.

What are the benefits of using Google Ads?

The benefits of using Google Ads include increased online visibility, targeted advertising, measurable results, control over ad spend, and the ability to reach potential customers at the right moment when they are actively searching for products or services.
